Indian Governance
Summit


Scroll Down

Public Policy and Opinion Cell organised the 3rd edition of its Flagship Event, "Indian Governance Summit-2022," first-time in-person mode from the 4th to the 6th of November 2022.

The summit provides a platform to discuss and deliberate on challenging issues of national importance, integrity and contemporary challenges in these domains. We invited economists, journalists, policymakers and government beurocrats for various talks and discussions. The notable bureaucrat, IITK alumni, 1984 batch IAS officer Mr Durga Shankar Mishra, presently Chief Secretary of Govt of UP, was our Chief Guest. Some prominent speakers include Dr Neeti Shikha, Associate Dean, ISPP and Mr Praveen Swamy, National Security Editor, The Print.

Our Speakers

  • Mr. Durga Shanker Mishra

    Hon'ble Chief Secretary, Uttar Pradesh

    Durga Shanker Mishra is a 1984 batch Indian Administrative Service officer of Uttar Pradesh cadre. He is the current Chief Secretary to Government of Uttar Pradesh. He was Housing and Urban Affairs Secretary and Chairman of the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation.

  • Dr. Manoj Kumar

    Founder & Managing Partner at Hammurabi &Solomon Partners

    Dr. Manoj Kumar is the Founder & Managing Partner of Hammurabi & Solomon Partners, a leading Full-Service Law firm headquartered in New Delhi. Law plays a crucial role in framing the public policy in a manner that it's beneficial for everyone and at the same time scales it up to the limits of our constitution.

  • Major General Anil Verma

    Head of ADR-Association for Democratic Reforms

    Maj. Gen. Anil Verma is the Head of ADR-Association for Democratic Reforms with immense experience on the subject.

  • Dr. Neeti Shikha

    Associate Dean. Indian School of Public Policy

    Dr. Neeti Shikha holds over a decade of experience in researching and teaching in the area of corporate laws and governance.The judiciary is one of the three essential but equal pillars of a modern democratic state.

  • Arun Maira

    Former Chairman of Boston Consulting Group

    Mr. Arun Maira is a management consultant and former member of Planning Commision of India. He is also a former Chairman of Boston Consulting Group,, India. He was the first non-bumiputera CEO in Malaysia. He was board member of Tata Motors from 1981 to 1989 and played an instrumental role in Tata Motors's entry into the LCV segment. He then worked at Arthur D. Little for 10 years where he was Leader of Global Organisation Practice and managing director of Innovation Associates, a subsidiary of Arthur D. Little.

  • Praveen Swamy

    National Security Editor, The Print

    Praveen Swami Sir is a renowned journalist and author. He specialized in International issues and strategy formation. He is currently the national security editor at The Print. He was the Associate Editor of the Indian newspaper The Hindu.
